With the ever-increasing impact of technology in our lives, it is essential for the younger generation to explore and understand its possibilities. TECHVVIZ promises an exciting platform for students to showcase their talents and exchange knowledge with each other. Please join us on Thursday, 1st August and Friday, 2nd August 2024 for TECHVVIZ. The events are open for students of Classes 9 to 12.
